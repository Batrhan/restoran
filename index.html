Description

The Loft Restaurant is a cozy place in the city center where you can enjoy fine cuisine and a pleasant atmosphere. The restaurant's menu includes dishes of European and Japanese cuisine, as well as a wide selection of drinks and desserts. Loft uses only fresh and quality ingredients, and the service is top notch. The restaurant is ideal for both business lunches and romantic dinners.

VIP Rooms

VIP rooms in the restaurant are cozy and private rooms designed to serve guests who value privacy and comfort. VIP rooms usually have everything you need for a business meeting or private dinner, such as armchairs, tables, audio and video equipment. In these rooms you can enjoy an exquisite menu and the best service in a private and comfortable environment.
